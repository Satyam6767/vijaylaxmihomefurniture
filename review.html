Looking for clarification? Ask us, and we'll gladly help!

Do you offer custom furniture designs?

Yes, we provide custom furniture design services to fit your specific requirements in terms of size, style, and material.

What is the delivery time for furniture orders?

Delivery times vary depending on the item. Typically, custom furniture takes 3-4 weeks for delivery, while in-stock items are delivered within 7-10 days.

Do you offer assembly services for the furniture?

Yes, we offer free assembly services for all furniture purchases. Our team will handle the installation at your home.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer a 15-day return or exchange policy for most furniture items, provided they are in original condition. Custom pieces are non-refundable.

Do you provide warranty on your furniture?

Yes, all our furniture comes with a 1-year warranty against manufacturing defects. Some items may have extended warranties depending on the manufacturer.

Can I track my furniture delivery?

Yes, we provide real-time tracking for all deliveries. Once your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking link via email or SMS.
